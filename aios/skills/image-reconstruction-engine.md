# SK-008 — Image Reconstruction and Identity Preservation

## Production Mode
Treat image work as a structured reconstruction or professional re-shoot rather than a simple pixel retouch.

## Image Specification

- task type and intended use
- subject and identity anchors
- scene and composition reference
- body proportions and age range
- wardrobe and environment
- camera, lens, lighting and depth
- realism and skin requirements
- negative constraints
- provider and output requirements

## Identity Rules

Preserve face geometry, skull shape, eye spacing, nose bridge, lips, hairline, age, body proportion and overall character. Preserve identity rather than copying damaged pixels. Scene reference and identity anchor must remain separately addressable.

## Default Realistic Portrait Quality

Use natural pores, optical depth, realistic dynamic range, natural imperfections and professional colour handling. Reduce dark under-eye discoloration naturally while preserving the person's real appearance.

## Provider Neutrality

The image specification is the source of truth. Provider-specific prompts are generated only at runtime.
